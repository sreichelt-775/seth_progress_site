Tank-scale tests

Plots and notes for the first ~1300 hours pulled on 2026-03-21.

Update summary

I switched out the GeoTac power supply that I was using for the RH sensors because Yuquan needed it. I replaced it with a Dr. Meter power source, and the RH sensors are now showing much less drift. There were only a couple of spikes on the newest plots shown below.

I plan to look into these more closely across all channels and confirm whether they remain within the calibration range of the sensors; if not, I will smooth them. The jump around hour 1100 was caused by switching the power source. Overall, the system appears to be stabilizing further, particularly at greater distances from the heat source.

Saturation is still decreasing closest to the heat source, and the RH sensors nearest the heat source appear more stable. I will pull and generate another set of plots next week, and then we can discuss increasing the heat in the coming weeks and preparing for hydration.
